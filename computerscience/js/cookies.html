‚ö° Quick Tips

  • LocalStorage: Use for client-side data that needs to persist (user settings, progress, etc).
  • Cookies: Small data (4KB max), often used by the server for sessions/authentication.
  • Cookies can have expires or max-age to control lifespan.
  • Cookies are sent to server with each request, Local Storage stays client-side only.
