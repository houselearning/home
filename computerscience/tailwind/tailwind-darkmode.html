Tailwind supports dark mode out of the box! You just add the dark variant to your classes and toggle dark mode by adding class="dark" on a parent element (usually <html> or <body>).

Tailwind has two popular dark mode strategies: 'media' (auto based on user system preferences) and 'class' (manual toggle). The 'class' strategy gives you full control over when dark mode activates.
