What is a Percent?

A **percent** means "per hundred." It's a way to express a number as a part of 100. The symbol is %.

You can write a percent in a few ways:

  • As a percent: **50%**
  • As a fraction: **$$\frac{50}{100}$$**
  • As a decimal: **0.50**

All three of these represent the same value!
