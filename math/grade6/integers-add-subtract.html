What are Integers?

Integers are all the whole numbers, including the negative whole numbers, and zero. For example: ..., -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3, ...

You can think of a number line as a visual tool for working with integers. The numbers to the right of zero are positive, and the numbers to the left of zero are negative.
