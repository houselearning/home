The Secret of Regrouping

Sometimes, a column adds up to more than 9, or a number is too small to subtract from! That's when we regroup (also known as carrying or borrowing).

Operation StrategyHow it Works (MathJax)
Carrying (Addition) If the ones place adds to $13$, keep the $3$ and carry the $1$ to the tens place.

$$ 458 + 225 = 683 $$
Borrowing (Subtraction) If you need to subtract $8$ from $5$, you must borrow $1$ ten from the neighbor!

$$ 5,432 - 1,218 = 4,214 $$
Place Value Alignment Always stack your numbers vertically by place value: Ones over Ones, Tens over Tens.
