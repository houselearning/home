Welcome to the Decimal Lab

Decimals are what happens when we break one whole into 10 or 100 equal parts. You see decimals every day when you look at prices! A dime is 0.1 of a dollar, and a penny is 0.01 of a dollar.


Decimal Place Value

The decimal point separates the whole numbers from the parts. As you move right from the point, the values get smaller and smaller!

[Image of decimal place value chart]
Ones . Tenths ($\frac{1}{10}$) Hundredths ($\frac{1}{100}$)
0 . 7 0

$0.70$ is the same as $0.7$ because 70 hundredths = 7 tenths!
