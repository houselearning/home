💵 Money Lesson: Coins & Cash

Introduction

Today we’ll learn about both coins and cash (bills)! This is the money we use to buy things.

  • 🟤 Penny = 1¢
  • 🔵 Nickel = 5¢
  • ⚪ Dime = 10¢
  • ⚫ Quarter = 25¢
  • 💵 $1 Bill = 100¢
  • 💵 $5 Bill = 500¢
  • 💵 $10 Bill = 1000¢
