Telling Time

The clock tells us when to eat, sleep, and go to school. Let's learn how it works!


The Hands of a Clock

A clock has two main hands:

  • Hour Hand: Short and slow. It tells the hour.
  • Minute Hand: Long and fast. At "o'clock," it points to 12.
