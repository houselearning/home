Intro & Concept

For **multiplication**, count the total decimal places in the numbers you are multiplying. The final answer will have that many decimal places. For **division**, move the decimal point in the divisor to make it a whole number, and move the decimal point in the dividend the same number of places. Then divide! 🧠
