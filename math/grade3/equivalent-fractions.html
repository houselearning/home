The Baker's Rule

Whatever you do to the top (Numerator), you MUST do to the bottom (Denominator). MathJax renders this rule perfectly:

\[ \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{3}{3} = \frac{3}{6} \]

"We multiplied both top and bottom by 3 to get an equivalent fraction!"
