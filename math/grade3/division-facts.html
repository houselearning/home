Welcome to the Division Lab

In multiplication, we combine groups. In division, we share things equally. If you have 10 cookies and 2 friends, division tells you how many cookies each friend gets!


The Inverse Strategy

Division is just multiplication in reverse. If you know that **$3 \times 4 = 12$**, then you already know that **$12 \div 3 = 4$**!
